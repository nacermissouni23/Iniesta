## What I do

Analyze corner kick data to determine effectiveness:

- Calculate corner-to-shot conversion rates (baseline: ~20.5%)
- Calculate shot-to-goal conversion from corners (baseline: ~1 in 9)
- Compute expected goals per corner (baseline: ~0.022 xG)
- Estimate how many games before a team scores from a corner (~1 per 10 games)
- Assess counter-attack risk when committing defenders forward

## Key findings from The Numbers Game

- Only 20.5% of corners lead to a shot on goal
- 89% of shots from corners are wasted (no goal)
- Average corner is worth ~0.022 goals
- Average PL team scores from a corner once every 10 games
- Net goal difference contribution is close to zero due to counter-attack risk
- Short corners to retain possession may be superior to delivering into the box

## When to use me

Use this skill when analyzing a team's set-piece effectiveness, evaluating whether to commit defenders forward at corners, or building a model to predict corner outcomes.
