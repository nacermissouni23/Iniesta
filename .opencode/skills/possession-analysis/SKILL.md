## What I do

Analyze team possession and passing patterns:

- Track pass completion rates by zone and direction
- Identify passing networks and preferred combinations
- Measure progressive vs. sideways/backward passing
- Calculate possession turnover rates and locations
- Evaluate build-up patterns through the thirds

## Key findings from The Numbers Game

- Average pass completion is ~50% (coin toss odds)
- Pass completion diminishes with each additional pass in a sequence
- Only ~8.5% of passing movements contain more than 3 passes
- ~50% of all goals come from regained possessions (turnovers)
- 30% of regains in the opponent's penalty area lead to shots
- The ball changes hands ~400 times per game on average

## When to use me

Use this skill when analyzing a team's possession style, evaluating build-up effectiveness, or identifying passing patterns and vulnerabilities in possession phases.
