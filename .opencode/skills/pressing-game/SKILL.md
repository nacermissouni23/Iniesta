## What I do

Analyze team pressing and defensive regain patterns:

- Measure pressing intensity by zone (high, mid, low block)
- Track regain locations and their conversion to shots/goals
- Evaluate counter-pressing after possession loss
- Calculate final third regain percentages
- Assess pressing triggers and defensive organization

## Key findings from The Numbers Game

- 30% of all regains in the opponent's penalty area lead to shots on goal
- ~50% of all goals come from regained possessions
- Ball changes hands ~400 times per game
- Pressing was first quantified by Charles Reep (though not invented by him)
- Barcelona and Spain's success was built on the pressing game
- Liverpool used final third regain percentage to evaluate signings (Downing, Henderson)

## When to use me

Use this skill when analyzing a team's defensive press, evaluating counter-pressing effectiveness, scouting players for their pressing contribution, or building a pressing intensity model.
