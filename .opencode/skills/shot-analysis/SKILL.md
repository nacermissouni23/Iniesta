## What I do

Analyze shooting data to measure finishing effectiveness:

- Calculate shot-to-goal conversion rates (baseline: ~1 in 9)
- Analyze shot locations and their expected value
- Evaluate individual and team finishing efficiency
- Identify shot patterns that lead to higher conversion
- Compare actual goals to expected goals (xG)

## Key findings from The Numbers Game

- ~1 in 9 shots yields a goal on average
- ~50% of all goals come from regained possessions
- Shots from regains in the opponent's penalty area have ~30% conversion to shots on goal
- Corner-to-goal conversion takes ~10 games per goal on average
- Stochastic process: which specific shot becomes a goal is random, but rates are stable

## When to use me

Use this skill when evaluating team or player finishing ability, building an xG model, analyzing match efficiency, or comparing actual vs. expected performance.
